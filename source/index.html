Welcome to the Official Website of SPCCAA NY

Welcome to the website of St Paul's Co-educational College (SPCC) Alumni Association (Greater New York Chapter). SPCCAA NY was founded by a group of devoted alumni in New York area. The association organizes a diverse mix of activities throughout the year catered to all members.

Please feel free to send your comments or suggestions to spccaany (at) gmail.com.
